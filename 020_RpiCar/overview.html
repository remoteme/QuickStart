RaspberryPi FPV Car

We will build 4wd car – the steering will be similar like in a tank (differential steering)– to turn, one side of wheels will rotate with different speed than other. A Raspberry Pi Camera is attached on the car using a pan-tilt mechanism. The robot will be controlled by web browser (Chrome or Firefox at desktop or mobile phone), at the browser, We will get a video stream (with only 200ms latency). Also we can control the car movements and camera position.
